Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpbee
Burpbees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squar and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position amd rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climbers
Mountain climbers are a good cardio excerise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain).
